Tankfest 2024!

Tankfest, what to expect.

TTankfest is an annual event that brings together military enthusiasts, history buffs, and families for a captivating exploration of armored warfare. Held at renowned tank museums or military bases, Tankfest showcases an impressive array of tanks and armored vehicles from different eras and nations. Visitors have the unique opportunity to witness dynamic tank displays, where these mighty machines rumble across the terrain, demonstrating their power and capabilities. Throughout the event, attendees can delve into interactive exhibits, engage with knowledgeable historians, and even experience tank rides firsthand. Tankfest serves as a vibrant celebration of military history and technology, offering a thrilling and educational experience for all ages.
